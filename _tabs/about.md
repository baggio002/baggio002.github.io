Hello and welcome to my tech blog!

I am a Senior Software Architect and veteran Java Engineer with over 14 years of experience in designing, building, and optimizing distributed software systems.

## 💡 The Story Behind This Blog
Despite having extensive experience, my journey in the North American tech industry has come with its share of challenges. Without a local educational background, I faced significant hurdles in technical interviews. The most frustrating part was often the language barrier—not general communication, but the specific challenge of mapping English technical jargon to my deep, existing knowledge base on the fly. Sometimes, words failed to capture the complex architectures I had clearly designed in my head.
I created this blog to bridge that gap. This space is my portfolio and my voice. I want to give potential interviewers and future colleagues a clearer, more comprehensive view of my technical capabilities, problem-solving skills, and architectural mindset, beyond what a fast-paced interview can reveal.
